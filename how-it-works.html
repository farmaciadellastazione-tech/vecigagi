How it works

🎯 Spaced Repetition (SM-2)

LinguaeQuiz uses the same algorithm as Anki. Every word has a level from 0 to 7. Answer correctly and the interval before it reappears doubles — from 1 day up to 6 months. Answer wrong and it comes back immediately. After a few sessions, the app knows which words you struggle with and focuses on those.

Lvl 0: today Lvl 1: +1 day Lvl 2: +3 days Lvl 3: +7 days Lvl 4: +14 days Lvl 5: +30 days Lvl 6: +90 days Lvl 7: +180 days
📈 CEFR Adaptive Difficulty

The app estimates your level from your performance and unlocks harder vocabulary progressively. Beginners see only A1 words. As you improve, A2, B1 and B2 vocabulary is introduced automatically.
